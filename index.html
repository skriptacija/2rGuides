About Us

Your Adventure Awaits

Hey there, fellow nature enthusiasts! We're Stjepan and Željko, your dynamic duo of adventure and exploration in the breathtaking Plitvice Lakes National Park. Oh, and in case you haven't caught on yet, yes, "2rguides" stands for "tworguides" – because, let's face it, we're double the fun and double the expertise!

Meet Stjepan

The Almost - Legend of Plitvice

I'm Stjepan, the seasoned veteran of Plitvice Lakes. With 15 years under my belt as a tour guide, I've practically become a local legend (or so they say). I know every nook, cranny, and stone in this park like the back of my hand – yet, somehow, I still manage to mistake a rock for a waterfall every now and then. But hey, who needs to tell the difference when you're having this much fun?

Guided Tours

Explore with 2RGUIDES

At 2rguides, we believe that every traveler deserves the chance to experience the natural wonders of Plitvice Lakes National Park – even if you're short on time. That's why we offer a variety of tour options designed to fit your schedule and preferences, all led by our expert team of local guides, Stjepan and Željko.

Half-Day Tour: A Taste of Plitvice in Just 4 Hours

Short on time but still eager to soak in the beauty of Plitvice Lakes? Our Half-Day Tour is the perfect option for you. Over the course of four unforgettable hours, we'll lead you on a whirlwind adventure through some of the park's most iconic sights and hidden gems. Led by Stjepan or Željko, our Half-Day Tour offers a condensed yet immersive experience, giving you a taste of everything that makes Plitvice Lakes so special. From the mesmerizing waterfalls to the lush forest trails, you'll get to witness firsthand the magic of this UNESCO World Heritage site – all with the expert guidance and insider knowledge of a local guide. Ideal for travelers with limited time or those looking to maximize their Plitvice Lakes experience in a single afternoon, our Half-Day Tour promises adventure, discovery, and memories that will last a lifetime.
