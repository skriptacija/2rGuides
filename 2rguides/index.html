your adventure awaits

Hey there, fellow nature enthusiasts! We're Stjepan and Željko, your dynamic duo of adventure and exploration in the breathtaking Plitvice Lakes National Park. Oh, and in case you haven't caught on yet, yes, "2rguides" stands for "tworguides" – because, let's face it, we're double the fun and double the expertise!

guided tours


Meet Stjepan:

The Almost - Legend of Plitvice

I'm Stjepan, the seasoned veteran of Plitvice Lakes. With 15 years under my belt as a tour guide, I've practically become a local legend (or so they say). I know every nook, cranny, and stone in this park like the back of my hand – yet, somehow, I still manage to mistake a rock for a waterfall every now and then. But hey, who needs to tell the difference when you're having this much fun?

And I'm Željko:

Nature's Number One Fanatic

And I'm the other half of this adventure-filled equation – Željko. As a nature lover, climber, mountaineer, and caver, you could say I'm just a tad obsessed with all things natural. Sometimes my adventurous spirit gets the best of me, and I find myself impulsively leaping off cliffs into the crystal-clear waters below. But hey, life's too short to play it safe all the time, right?




explore with 2rguides

At 2rguides, we believe that every traveler deserves the chance to experience the natural wonders of Plitvice Lakes National Park – even if you're short on time. That's why we offer a variety of tour options designed to fit your schedule and preferences, all led by our expert team of local guides, Stjepan and Željko.


Half-Day Tour: A Taste of Plitvice in Just 4 Hours

Short on time but still eager to soak in the beauty of Plitvice Lakes? Our Half-Day Tour is the perfect option for you. Over the course of four unforgettable hours, we'll lead you on a whirlwind adventure through some of the park's most iconic sights and hidden gems.

Led by Stjepan or Željko, our Half-Day Tour offers a condensed yet immersive experience, giving you a taste of everything that makes Plitvice Lakes so special. From the mesmerizing waterfalls to the lush forest trails, you'll get to witness firsthand the magic of this UNESCO World Heritage site – all with the expert guidance and insider knowledge of a local guide.

Ideal for travelers with limited time or those looking to maximize their Plitvice Lakes experience in a single afternoon, our Half-Day Tour promises adventure, discovery, and memories that will last a lifetime.

Full-Day Tour: Dive Deep into the Beauty of Plitvice Lakes

Are you ready for the ultimate Plitvice Lakes adventure? Our Full-Day Tour is tailor-made for those who want to immerse themselves fully in the breathtaking beauty of this natural wonderland. Led by our expert guides, Stjepan and Željko, this comprehensive tour ensures you won't miss a single corner or hidden gem of the park.

From the crack of dawn until the sun sets, we'll lead you on a captivating journey through Plitvice Lakes National Park. Traverse winding trails, marvel at cascading waterfalls, and wander through lush forests as you soak in the serenity and splendor of this UNESCO World Heritage site.

Ideal for adventurers and nature lovers alike, our Full-Day Tour offers ample time to explore each mesmerizing vista and discover the park's rich biodiversity. Whether you're captivated by the vivid hues of the lakes, enchanted by the tranquility of the untouched wilderness, or simply eager to bask in the beauty of nature, this immersive experience promises a day of unforgettable memories.

So pack your sense of wonder and join us for a full day of exploration, inspiration, and pure natural bliss at Plitvice Lakes National Park.

Hiking Tours: Discover the Hidden Gems of Plitvice Lakes

Ready to venture off the beaten path and explore the untouched wilderness of Plitvice Lakes National Park? Our Hiking Tours offer a unique opportunity to immerse yourself in the rich biodiversity and pristine beauty of this natural treasure.

While the lakes and waterfalls may steal the spotlight, did you know that over 80 percent of the park is cloaked in dense forests, including some classified as virgin? Our Hiking Tours take you deep into these hidden realms, where towering trees, tranquil streams, and abundant wildlife await.

Čorkova Uvala Trail: A Journey into the Heart of the Virgin Forest

For the intrepid explorer seeking a true wilderness experience, the Čorkova Uvala Trail is an adventure like no other. Stretching 20 kilometers through remote and untouched terrain, this epic hiking route leads you to the edge of the virgin forest, far from the hustle and bustle of the crowded lakeside trails.

Prepare to be awed by the pristine beauty of nature as you traverse rugged terrain, cross babbling brooks, and ascend to breathtaking viewpoints. With Stjepan and Željko as your guides, you'll embark on a journey of discovery and wonder, uncovering the secrets of Plitvice Lakes' hidden wilderness.

Plitvice Trails: A Shortcut to Serenity

Prefer a shorter, more leisurely hike? No problem! Our Plitvice Trails option offers a condensed yet equally beautiful alternative to the full Čorkova Uvala Trail. Perfect for those who want to experience the tranquility of the forest without committing to a full day of hiking, this route showcases the best of Plitvice's natural splendor in a more manageable package.

Whether you choose the rugged adventure of Čorkova Uvala or the serene beauty of Plitvice Trails, our Hiking Tours promise an unforgettable journey through the untouched wilderness of Plitvice Lakes National Park. Lace up your boots, breathe in the fresh mountain air, and let the adventure begin!

Rastoke Village Tour: Step into a Fairy Tale

Escape the hustle and bustle of modern life and journey back in time with our Rastoke Village Tour. Nestled at the confluence of the Slunjčica and Korana rivers, this picturesque village exudes charm and nostalgia, offering a glimpse into Croatia's rich cultural heritage.

Spend your morning or afternoon strolling along cobblestone streets and marveling at the quaint wooden houses that line the riverbanks. With its cascading waterfalls, lush greenery, and rustic charm, Rastoke village feels like something straight out of a fairy tale.

Immerse yourself in the history and traditions of this historic millers' village as you explore its winding pathways and hidden nooks. Visit one of the few remaining and operating mills in the area, where you can witness firsthand the age-old craft of milling and gain insight into the way of life in days gone by.

Feeling adventurous? Extend your Rastoke experience with a full-day hike led by Stjepan and Željko. Follow the path of the Slunjčica river all the way to its source, where crystal-clear waters beckon you to dip your toes and refresh your spirit. It's the perfect way to immerse yourself fully in the natural beauty of the area and create memories that will last a lifetime.

So why wait? Embark on a journey through time and nature with our Rastoke Village Tour. Whether you're seeking history, tranquility, or a touch of adventure, this enchanting village has something for everyone. Join us as we explore the magic of Rastoke and uncover the secrets of its timeless allure.

Craving a thrilling off-road adventure through rugged terrain? We'll make it happen. From scenic hikes to adrenaline-pumping activities, the choice is yours.

With Stjepan and Željko at the helm, you can trust that your custom trail experience will be nothing short of extraordinary. Our expert guides will work closely with you to design a bespoke itinerary that caters to your interests, preferences, and sense of adventure.

So go ahead, dream big and let your imagination run wild. With our Custom Trails option, there's no limit to what you can explore, discover, and experience. Whether you're seeking thrills, tranquility, or a little bit of both, we'll make sure your custom trail adventure is nothing short of unforgettable.
